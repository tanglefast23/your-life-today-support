# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 13, 2026**

Your Life Today ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information:** Email address (if you sign in with Apple or email)
- **User-generated content:** Tasks, notes, habits, financial records, travel destinations, weather cities, and research alerts you create
- **Device information:** Device type and operating system version for crash reporting

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not collect advertising identifiers
- We do not track your location history (location is used only for real-time weather)
- We do not access your health data beyond what you explicitly grant (step count, sleep, workouts for habit tracking)
- We do not read your calendar events — they are fetched locally on your device

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and improve the app experience
- To sync your data across devices via secure cloud storage (Supabase)
- To send notifications you have opted into

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data.

## Your Rights

- **Export:** Download all your data from Settings → Data & Privacy → Download My Data
- **Delete:** Permanently delete your account and all data from Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete My Account
- **Manage subscriptions:** Via your Apple ID settings

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** — Cloud database and authentication
- **RevenueCat** — Subscription management
- **Sentry** — Crash reporting
- **Apple HealthKit** — Health data (only with your explicit permission)

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/tanglefast23/your-life-today-support/issues/new) or email yourlifetodayapp@gmail.com.
