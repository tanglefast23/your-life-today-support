## Get Help

- **Report a bug or request a feature:** [Open an Issue](https://github.com/tanglefast23/your-life-today-support/issues/new)
- **Email:** yourlifetodayapp@gmail.com

## FAQ

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Your Life Today → Cancel.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
Yes! Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Download My Data.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete My Account. This permanently removes all your data.

## Privacy

Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.
